# School Equipment Lending Portal: Video Demo Script

**Estimated Duration**: 4-5 Minutes
**Objective**: To demonstrate the role-based workflow for borrowing and managing school equipment within a premium, cinematic interface.

---

## 1. Introduction (0:00 - 0:45)
**[Visual: Show the Landing Page (`/`)]**
- **Speaker**: "Welcome to the School Equipment Lending Portal. This platform is designed to streamline how students and staff borrow lab gear, electronics, and sports equipment."
- **Action**: Scroll through the landing page.
- **Speaker**: "Notice the premium cinematic design—what we call the 'Dream Edition.' We've used a bronze and gold palette with subtle animations and glassmorphism to create a high-end educational experience."

## 2. Role-Based Registration (0:45 - 1:30)
**[Visual: Navigate to `/signup`]**
- **Speaker**: "The portal uses Role-Based Access Control. When signing up, users can select whether they are a Student, Staff member, or Admin."
- **Action**: Fill in mock details and select 'Student' as the role.
- **Speaker**: "Each role has a tailored experience. Students focus on borrowing, while Staff focus on inventory management."

## 3. Student Workflow: Requesting Items (1:30 - 2:30)
**[Visual: Dashboard as a Student]**
- **Speaker**: "As a Student, my dashboard is an interactive inventory. I can see what's available and what's out of stock in real-time."
- **Action**: Click 'Request Item' on a piece of equipment and add a note like 'For Physics Lab'.
- **Speaker**: "Submitting a request is seamless. The item's availability is tracked, and I can monitor my request status on the 'My Requests' page."

## 4. Staff Workflow: Inventory & Approvals (2:30 - 3:45)
**[Visual: Logout and Login as a Staff member]**
- **Speaker**: "Now, let's switch to a Staff member. Notice the 'Management' tab in the sidebar—this is only visible to Staff and Admins."
- **Action**: Click on 'Management'.
- **Speaker**: "Staff can add new equipment to the school's inventory or delete obsolete items. Most importantly, they can see all incoming student requests and approve or reject them with a single click."

## 5. System Integrity & Conclusion (3:45 - 4:15)
**[Visual: Show the 'My Requests' page updated to 'Approved']**
- **Speaker**: "The entire system is powered by a robust Node.js backend and PostgreSQL. We've ensured that all data—from user roles to item conditions—is perfectly synced."
- **Speaker**: "Thank you for watching the School Equipment Lending Portal demo."
